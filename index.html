HOW TO PLAY

⚡The glowing pad is your power grid. It feeds the whole city.
👆Tap a building to run a wire and charge it. Tap again to send more power — it fills faster.
🔋Every building drains over time. Some fast (factories), some slow (houses). Don't let the bar hit zero.
📊Each grid powers only a few at once (the X/Y on it). When one's full, divert supply to one running low.
⚡3 chances. A blackout costs one (hospital costs 2). Fill a Power Cell for +1 chance.
🏆Survive as long as you can. A 2nd grid and more buildings arrive as you go.
